Mission Statement

To produce thoughtful natural gardens and land restorations with healthy soils that provide food and habitat for local flora and fauna using low disturbance and rainwater harvesting techniques.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: Every decision prioritizes long-term environmental health
  • Community: We strengthen neighborhoods through shared green spaces
  • Education: Empowering clients with knowledge for lasting stewardship
  • Excellence: Craftsmanship and attention to detail in every project
